Book Alison Cheperdak for Keynotes, Trainings, & Events

Press

Alison is a sought after thought leader, keynote speaker, and podcast guest ready to discuss and train on a variety of topics including etiquette, executive presence, public speaking, personal branding, media training, leadership, and respect in dynamic, diverse environments.  

Alison brings her experience as a respected attorney, former nonprofit executive, and television news anchor to inspire diverse audiences from interns to executives. 

She travels nationally and internationally as a keynote speaker and would be delighted to join your organization.  

As a motivational speaker, Alison believes anyone can increase their confidence, poise, and positive impact with the right tools and proper encouragement.
