Frequently Asked Questions

1. Are these courses a good fit if I’m a beginner?

Yes! Our courses are designed to meet you where you are, whether you’re just starting out or looking to refine advanced skills. For beginners, we focus on building a strong foundation in etiquette and professional skills, providing practical tips and actionable steps to help you feel confident in any situation.

2. Can trainings be customized?

Absolutely! All our trainings are fully customizable to address the specific goals and challenges of your organization or individual needs.

3. Is the Dining Etiquette Demystified course suitable for
beginners?

Yes, the Dining Etiquette Demystified course is perfect for anyone who wants to master modern fine dining and table manners. Whether you're new to formal dining or just looking for a refresher, this course will help you navigate with confidence.

4.  Are these courses available online?

Yes, all of our courses are available online, making it convenient for clients around the globe to access our expertise.

5. Do you offer private one-on-one sessions?
 

Yes, we offer private coaching for individuals who want a tailored experience. This option is especially popular for our Polished Professional and Modern Manners courses.

7. How do you improve someone's executive presence?

We use specific, practical activities to help our clients strengthen their executive presence such as mock presentations, role playing scenarios, posture and body language exercises, personal styling consultations, speech refinement, networking simulations, and media training. These activities are tailored to individual needs and designed to build confidence, credibility, and a commanding presence in professional settings.

8. Do you offer etiquette training for children?

Yes, we work with all ages! We provide etiquette training designed specifically for children, focusing on skills such as greetings, dining etiquette, and modeling good manners in everyday situations.

formal dinners?

Yes, we work with all ages and can prepare children for special events by teaching them dining etiquette, social graces, and how to feel confident in formal settings.

Yes, we work with all ages, including teenagers! Whether they’re preparing for college interviews, social events, or their first jobs, we can help them develop confidence and polished etiquette skills.

12. What is included in a social media audit, and how do I know if it's a good fit for me?

A social media audit includes a comprehensive review of your profiles across platforms. We assess your content, tone, visuals, and engagement to ensure they align with your personal or professional brand. You’ll receive detailed feedback and actionable recommendations to elevate your online presence, whether you’re building a career, growing a business, or simply looking to make a great impression.

A social media audit is a great fit for anyone who wants to:
  • Enhance their personal or professional brand.
  • Ensure their online presence is polished and consistent.
  • Align their social media content with specific career or personal goals.
  • Improve engagement and audience connection.

Whether you’re an individual, entrepreneur, or organization, this service can help you stand out and put your best digital foot forward!
