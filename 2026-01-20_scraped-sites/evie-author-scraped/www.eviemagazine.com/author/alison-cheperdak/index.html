Alison Cheperdak

Alison M. Cheperdak, J.D., is the founder of Elevate Etiquette, where she teaches modern manners in a gracious and grounded way. Drawing on years of experience as a TV news reporter, an attorney in the public, private, and nonprofit sectors, and a White House staffer, Alison blends her professional background with her love of all things thoughtful—whether she’s hosting friends for dinner, exploring new cultures, renovating historic homes with her husband, or reading her way through a rotating stack of classics, new releases, and everything in between.

She is the author of the forthcoming book "Was It Something I Said? Everyday Etiquette to Avoid Awkward Moments in Relationships, Work, and Life."
